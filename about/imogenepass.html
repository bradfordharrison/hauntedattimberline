About Imogene Pass

Return to Imogene Pass main page
Imogene Pass connects Ouray and Telluride. The top photo below shows the view north toward Ouray. The bottom photo shows the view south toward Telluride. If you look really closely at the bottom photo you can see the Tomboy Mine site and, just below that, the Smuggler-Union Mine tram house (click on the image to enlarge). The opening photo shows an alpine lake and abandoned house near the pass.
